##### Fourier Transform
Fouriertransformation af $x(t)$
$$
X(f)=F(x(t))=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} x(t)e^{-j_{2}\pi ft} \, dt
$$

Invers Fouriertransformation af $X(f)$:

$$
x(t)=F^{-1}(X(f))=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} X(f)e^{j_{2}\pi ft} \, df =
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(x)\cdot e^{-j\omega x} \, dx 
$$
