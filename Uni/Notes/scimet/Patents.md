## Summary

Patents are legal instruments that protect technical inventions by granting exclusive rights for a limited period.

> **Definition:**  
> A form of intellectual property that grants the holder exclusive rights to exploit a novel, non-obvious, and useful invention.
^def

## Core Requirements

- Novelty
- Inventive step (non-obviousness)
- Industrial applicability

## Purpose

- Incentivize innovation
- Enable commercialization
- Facilitate knowledge disclosure

## Relation to Scientific Practice

- Distinct from academic publication
- Public disclosure without protection may prevent patenting
- Raises ethical and strategic considerations
