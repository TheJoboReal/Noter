## Summary

Inferential statistics use sample data to draw conclusions about a population under uncertainty.

**Definition:**  


> Statistical methods that estimate population properties and test hypotheses using sample data.
^def

## Core Ideas

- Sampling uncertainty
- Probability-based reasoning
- Generalization from sample to population

## Role in Scientific Method

- Enables hypothesis testing
- Quantifies confidence in conclusions
- Requires representative sampling
