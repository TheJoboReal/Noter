## Summary

Descriptive statistics summarize and characterize the main features of a dataset without making inferences beyond the observed data.

**Definition:**  

> Methods for organizing, summarizing, and visualizing data to describe its central tendency and variability.
^def

## Core Measures

- Central tendency (mean, median, mode)
- Dispersion (variance, standard deviation, range)
- Distribution shape

## Role in Scientific Method

- First step in data analysis
- Used to inspect data quality and structure
- Does not support generalization beyond the sample
