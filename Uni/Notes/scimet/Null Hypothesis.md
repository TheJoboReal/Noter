## Summary

The null hypothesis represents the default assumption that no effect or difference exists in the population.

**Definition:**  

> A hypothesis stating that there is no systematic relationship or difference between variables.
^def

## Purpose

- Serves as a baseline for statistical testing
- Assumed true unless evidence suggests otherwise
