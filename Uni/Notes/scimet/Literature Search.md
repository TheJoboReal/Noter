## Summary

Literature search is the systematic process of identifying, evaluating, and organizing existing scientific work relevant to a research question.

**Definition:**  

> A structured method for locating and assessing prior research to establish context, avoid duplication, and ground new studies in existing knowledge.
^def

## Core Goals
- Identify state of the art
- Understand prior methods and results
- Refine research questions and hypotheses

## Key Activities
- Keyword selection
- Database searching
- Screening for relevance and quality
- Backward and forward citation tracking

## Evaluation Criteria
- Relevance to research question
- Methodological quality
- Publication venue and credibility
