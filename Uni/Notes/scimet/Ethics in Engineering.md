## Summary

Ethics in engineering concerns the moral responsibilities associated with designing, deploying, and evaluating technological systems.

> **Definition:**  
> The application of ethical principles to engineering decisions, considering their societal, environmental, and human impact.
^def

## Core Principles

- Responsibility for consequences
- Respect for human rights and privacy
- Transparency and accountability
- Safety and risk minimization

## Ethical Dimensions in Research

- Data collection and consent
- Bias and fairness
- Dual-use technologies
- Environmental impact

## Role in Scientific Method

- Shapes acceptable research practices
- Constrains experimental design choices
- Guides evaluation beyond technical success
