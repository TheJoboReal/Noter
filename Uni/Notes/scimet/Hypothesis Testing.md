## Summary

Hypothesis testing is a formal procedure for evaluating evidence about a hypothesis using statistical reasoning.

**Definition:**  

> A statistical framework for deciding whether observed data provide sufficient evidence to reject a null hypothesis.
^def

## General Procedure

1. Formulate hypotheses (H₀ and alternative)
2. Choose a statistical test
3. Compute test statistic and p-value
4. Decide whether to reject H₀

## Role in Scientific Method

- Links data to theory
- Supports falsification
- Requires careful interpretation
