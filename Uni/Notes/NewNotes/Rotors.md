#### Definition
Rotors generate:
	T: thrust perpendicular to propeller plane.
	M: reaction moment due to propeller and motor shaft rotation

Simple mathematical Model:
$$
M=k_{M}\Omega^2
$$
and:
$$
T=k_{T}\Omega^2
$$

$k_{T},k_{m}$ constant, $\Omega$ angular velocity of propeller and $k_{T}$ and $k_{M}$ and determined experimentally. Where $\Omega$ is rugulated by and ESC.
