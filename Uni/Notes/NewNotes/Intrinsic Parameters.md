##### Camera Matrix
$$
K=\begin{bmatrix}
f_{x} & \gamma & c_{x} \\
0 & f_{y} & c_{y} \\
0 & 0 & 1
\end{bmatrix}
$$
where $\gamma$ is not used much anymore due to camera technology improving drastically.
